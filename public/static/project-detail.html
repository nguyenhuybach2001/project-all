Giới thiệu sản phẩm

Idea: Climate change is the cause of natural phenomena such as floods and drought. Long periods of hot and sunny weather could result in various serious consequences. The forest fire in Australia, for instance, has burned 10 million hectares of land, damaged roundly 2000 houses, and 4000 constructions. About 1 billion wild animals were killed or injured by the fire. Additionally, the fire also released about 400 million tons of C02 and other pollutants into the atmosphere.

Description: The product describes the brave actions of firefighters and volunteers to put out the fire and save animals. The product gives a meaningful message to everybody: PROTECT GREEN LUNGS, PROTECT THE WORLD OF WILDLIFE.

Knowledge: Use drag-and-drop commands in the Scratch programming language. Use information about Australian facts and plant growth cycles. Apply the loop, next costume, message, and so on for each character.
